# recipe for irish soda bread

### ingredients:

* flours
* yogurt
* oatmeal
* roasted wheat germ
* baking soda

### directions:

preheat the oven, mix stuff together, knead for one minute, form into balls on floured baking sheet & let rise. cut nice x's with a chef knife. _bake until it sounds hollow!_
